GameZoid is the ultimate destination for playing, discussing, and creating games.

Features

We are constantly working to bring new updates and features to our platform, such as:

Steam Chat

Talk with friends or groups via text or voice without leaving GameZoid. Videos, Tweets, GIFs and more are supported; use wisely.

Game Hubs

Everything about your game, all in one place. Join discussions, upload content, and be the first to know about new updates.

Purchases Made Easy

Our storefront supports 100+ payment methods across over 35 currencies, giving you the flexibility to pay how you want.

Controller Support

GameZoid encourages developers to include controller support in their games including PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo controllers.

Early Access to Games

Discover, play, and get involved with games as they evolve. Be the first to see what's coming and become part of the process.

Available on Mobile

Access GameZoid anywhere from your iOS or Android device with the GameZoid mobile app.

Watch Tournaments

Watch online tournaments live with the click of a button, and share your game with friends or the rest of the community.

Seemless Playing

Create, discover, and download player-created mods and cosmetics for nearly 1,000 supported games.

Multilingual

Creating a global community is important to us, that's why our client supports 28 languages and counting.

And so much more...

Earn achievements, read reviews, explore custom recommendations, and more.
